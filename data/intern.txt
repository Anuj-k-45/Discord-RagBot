## Internship Overview
The AI Internship Program is a structured training program designed to help interns build practical skills in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and software development.

## Internship Duration
The internship lasts for 12 weeks. Each week focuses on a specific learning goal, including theory, hands-on projects, and assessments.

## Working Schedule
Interns are expected to work five days a week. The daily schedule is flexible, allowing interns to manage their time effectively while meeting weekly goals.

## Learning Format
The program includes a combination of recorded video lectures, live sessions, hands-on coding exercises, and project-based learning.

## Office Hours
Office hours are conducted during the first three weeks of the internship. These sessions allow interns to ask questions, clarify doubts, and receive guidance from mentors.

## Project-Based Learning
Interns are required to complete multiple projects throughout the program. These projects are designed to reinforce concepts such as data preprocessing, model training, and deployment.

## Evaluation and Feedback
Intern performance is evaluated through assignments, quizzes, and project submissions. Feedback is provided regularly to support continuous improvement.

## Certification
Interns who successfully complete all program requirements will receive a certificate of completion at the end of the internship.

## Support and Communication
All official communication, announcements, and discussions take place through Discord. Interns are encouraged to actively participate in the community.

## Code of Conduct
Interns are expected to maintain professionalism, follow ethical guidelines, and respect fellow participants throughout the program.
